## Ingredients
- 4–6 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt or mayo
- Salt + pepper
- Optional: mustard, paprika, chopped pickles
- Optional: lettuce cups or cucumber rounds

## Directions
1. Chop eggs.
2. Mix with yogurt/mayo + seasoning.
3. Spoon into lettuce cups (or eat with a fork).

> [!TIP] Low appetite portion
> Make 2–3 bites your goal. You can always come back later.
